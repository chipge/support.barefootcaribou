Setup Guide

  1. Lay the organizer flat and unzip all compartments.
  2. Fill each section and zip closed to compress.
  3. Wrap around your luggage and secure straps.

FAQ

Will it fit carry-on sizes?

Most carry-on sizes are supported. Check the size guide for details.

How do I clean the organizer?

Spot clean with mild soap and let air dry.

Can I use it on hard-shell luggage?

Yes. Tighten straps evenly to keep it secure.
