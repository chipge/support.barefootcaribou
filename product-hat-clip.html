FAQ

Why is my hat slipping?

Check the clip tension and ensure the brim is fully secured.

Can I use the clip on backpacks?

Yes. Use a sturdy strap and verify the hat is balanced.

What is the best way to clean the clip?

Wipe with a damp cloth and let it air dry.
