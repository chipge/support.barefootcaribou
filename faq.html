Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about orders, shipping, returns, warranty, and support.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

We offer $7.96 standard shipping with an estimated 5-12 day window. Expedited 2-5 day shipping is also available.

How do I track my order?

Use the tracking link from your shipping confirmation email or contact support for help.

Can I update my shipping address after ordering?

Contact support as soon as possible. Cancellations or changes must be requested before your order is dispatched from our fulfillment center.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return window?

We have a 14-day return policy from the date your item is received. Items must be unworn or unused, with tags, in original packaging, and include proof of purchase.

What does the warranty cover?

Warranty details will be posted soon. For now, contact support with your order number and issue details so we can review your case.

How do I start a return or warranty claim?

Gather your order number and product details, then contact support and include photos plus a short description when relevant. If approved, we will send return instructions.

Support

How quickly will I receive a response?

We respond within 1 business day. Live support is available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm PT.

What details should I include in my ticket?

Include your order number, a short issue summary, and any photos that show the issue.

How can I contact support directly?

Reach us by phone at +1 (888) 345-1452, email at support@barefootcaribou.com, live chat during business hours, or the support form on the contact page.
