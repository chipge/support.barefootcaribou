FAQ

Is the bag heat-safe?

Use only on cooled tools. Hot tools may damage liners.

How do I clean the interior?

Wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap, then air dry.

Can I store cords inside?

Yes. Coil cords loosely to avoid strain.
