Hair Tools Travel Bag FAQs

What tools fit inside this bag?

This bag measures 15.5" x 5" x 5" inches and is designed to fit most:

  • Flat irons
  • Curling irons
  • Curling wands
  • Hair straighteners
  • Heat brushes

If your tool exceeds 15.5 inches in length, it may not fit properly.

Can I place hot tools directly inside the bag?

The interior features a heat-resistant mat lining, allowing you to:

  • Set down hot tools during styling
  • Store tools before they are fully cooled

For safety, always turn tools off before storing.

What is the bag made from?

The exterior is made from heavy-duty, double-layered neoprene, which is:

  • Soft
  • Durable
  • Wear-resistant
  • Heat-resistant
  • Travel-friendly
How many interior pockets are included?

The bag includes:

  • Two interior pockets
  • One zippered pocket
  • Two elastic band sections (ideal for clips and small accessories)

This helps keep cords and accessories organized while traveling.

Can I use this while traveling?

Yes. The compact 15.5" design fits easily inside carry-on luggage and helps keep tools organized and separated from clothing.

Why doesn’t my oversized curling iron fit?

This bag accommodates tools up to 15.5 inches in length. Extra-long or salon-length tools may exceed this capacity.

How do I clean the bag?
  • Wipe clean with a damp cloth and mild soap.
  • Air dry completely before storing tools.
  • Do not machine wash.
Troubleshooting

The magnetic closure isn’t staying shut.

Ensure the bag is not overfilled. Excess bulk may prevent the magnet from aligning properly.

My tool feels tight inside the bag.

Verify that the tool length does not exceed 15.5 inches and that cords are wrapped neatly.

The bag has a slight scent when new.

New neoprene may have a mild manufacturing scent. Air the bag out for 24 hours before use.
